New

See where your spend goesCost tracking

Local-first cost, computed from the session files already on your Mac — across Claude, Codex, and Gemini. Break it down by project, over today / 7 days / month / all, and set a monthly budget per project with an alert before you blow past it.

ai-usage-bar · cost dashboard

↑ Live, interactive demo — switch the time window, toggle Cost / Tokens, click a project to filter, or flip to the menu-bar view. Open it full-screen →
Sample data. The real app reads your own local files — nothing leaves your Mac.

🔒 Private by design

Usage comes from your own Keychain token to one Anthropic endpoint; cost is computed entirely on-device from your local session files. No accounts, no analytics, no telemetry.

🪶 Tiny & native

A single Swift menu-bar app — AppKit + SwiftUI. No Electron, no background bloat. Reads what's already on disk.

🛠 Self-hosted

MIT-licensed and open source. Build it yourself with swift run; nothing is downloaded from anyone else.

Install

Two lines, no Xcode needed

$curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/captainkie/ai-usage-bar/main/install.sh | bash
$brew install captainkie/tap/ai-usage-bar

The ● 5h · wk item appears in your menu bar. Requires macOS 13+ and a signed-in Claude Code.
